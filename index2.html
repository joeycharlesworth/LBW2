Lisbon Blockchain Week


Join the Fight For Freedom

Lisbon Blockchain Week is a decentralized community-organized initiative dedicated to the enhancement of freedom, privacy and trust. There is no single owner, being an agnostic movement towards the premise that self-organization is the backbone of our ecosystem.


Want to host your own event, gathering or workshop?

You are free to do so. The concept is straightforward; keep it educational, try to avoid doing it at the same time as similar events and keep it "cost friendly".
